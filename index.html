Hello!

I'm Laura, an Irish lady living in London. I currently work for an agency called Digital Detox as a front end developer.

I completed my MA in Web Design & Content Planning at the University of Greenwich, London, in October 2012. During the twelve month course, I grew to really enjoy the challenges of learning to code, and gaining new skills with every project. Below, you can see a selection of projects that I completed throughout the course. You can also visit my coursework website here.

Before undertaking my masters, I graduated from Dublin City University with a BSc(Hons) in Multimedia, and then took a gap year to travel, which included a stint teaching English in Tokyo, to some adorable pre-schoolers.

When I'm not building websites, I enjoy going to gigs, riding my bike, and exploring London. I also enjoy getting involved with the web design community, and try to regularly attend conferences and meetups.
